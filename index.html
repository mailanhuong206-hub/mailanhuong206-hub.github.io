Sample Lesson Preview

A realistic workplace scenario with vocabulary, dialogue, and practice.

Situation

Scheduling a delivery update

Learn how to ask for a shipment status, confirm a delivery date, and respond professionally.

Vocabulary
  • 发货fā huòship / dispatch
  • 到货dào huòarrive / in stock
  • 预计yù jìestimated
Dialogue
You
请问这批货什么时候发货？
When will this batch ship?
Partner
预计明天发货，后天到货。
Estimated to ship tomorrow, arrive the day after.
